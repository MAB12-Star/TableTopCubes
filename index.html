Our Retro AR Collection — TableTop Cubes, Pongo, and Cube Snake — reimagines beloved 80s-style classics in immersive 3D Augmented Reality (AR). Each game transforms your environment into a fully interactive space where nostalgia meets innovation.

TableTop Cubes brings a new dimension to the timeless puzzle game, letting you stack, rotate, and clear shapes right on your coffee table in full 3D. Designed for solo play, it blends the challenge of traditional gameplay with a modern AR twist, allowing you to manipulate and interact with pieces as if they were physically present.

Pongo takes the legendary arcade paddle game and reimagines it in AR — you’ll rally a glowing energy ball across a floating arena that expands and contracts as you level up. With smooth physics and dynamic visuals, it’s an addictive and visually striking rework of a true classic.

Cube Snake revives the iconic snake gameplay with vibrant block-style motion in a 3D world, challenging your reflexes as you grow and navigate through an interactive grid. Each movement feels alive as the game blends precision and speed with immersive spatial effects.

Together, these titles celebrate the joy of retro gaming through cutting-edge AR technology, delivering fast, fun, and visually captivating experiences built in Unity and powered by the Meta SDK.

Features

  • 3D AR Gameplay: Experience Tetris like never before with a moveable 3D game board that appears right on your coffee table. Intuitive Controls: Easily adjust and interact with the game pieces using simple hand gestures.
  • Solo Play: Enjoy the challenge of beating your own high scores and improving your skills.
  • Dynamic Challenges: Each game is unique, with pieces that move and fit together with precision, ensuring a challenging and fun experience every time.
  • Built using Unity, the META SDK, and Avatar SDK, TableTop Cubes leverages the latest in AR technology to deliver an unparalleled gaming experience. Get ready to rediscover the fun of Tetris in a whole new dimension!
