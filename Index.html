Table Top Tetris

TableTop Cubes is an innovative twist on a classic game, bringing the timeless puzzle into a captivating 3D augmented reality (AR) experience. Designed for solo play, TableTop Cubes transforms your coffee table into a dynamic, interactive gaming platform. Players can enjoy the traditional gameplay, but with a modern AR twist that allows for a fully immersive experience. The game board appears in front of you in 3D, enabling you to manipulate and interact with the pieces as if they were physically present. Whether you're a long-time fan of Tetris or new to the game, TableTop Cubes offers an engaging and refreshing way to play.
